About Us

Established in 2015 in Taif, Saudi Arabia, Almanjal has grown to become a trusted leader in the distribution and marketing of premium feed additives and veterinary products. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, we work closely with global manufacturers to bring world-class solutions to the local market. Our expertise ensures that every product we supply meets the highest standards of safety, effectiveness, and reliability. By focusing on animal health and nutrition, we aim to support farmers, veterinarians, and pet owners with products that enhance growth, productivity, and overall well-being of livestock and companion animals.

Our Mission

Almanjal is dedicated to providing premium-quality feed additives and veterinary products that ensure the health and well-being of animals. Our mission is to source and import the finest products from trusted international manufacturers, carefully selecting each item to meet the highest standards of safety and effectiveness. By offering reliable and innovative solutions, we aim to support farmers, veterinarians, and animal owners in improving productivity, animal care, and overall farm efficiency. Through our strong commitment to quality and service, we strive to be a trusted partner in enhancing animal health and contributing to the growth of the local livestock industry.

Services

We specialize in the premium distribution of high-quality feed additives and veterinary medicines essential for maintaining animal health. Through global sourcing and import, we carefully select and bring in the best products from trusted international manufacturers. Our efficient logistics and delivery network ensures timely and secure supply. With strong technical expertise, our team offers valuable guidance on product usage and animal care. We provide market-driven solutions to meet the evolving needs of the animal health industry.
